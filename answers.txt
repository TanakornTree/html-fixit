1. What does the `title` tag do?
render the titile of the webpage

2. What information do images need, and why?
src => to reference the images shown
alt => to show keywords when error

3. What's the difference between `head` and `body`?

- head and body are the child element that html can have
- everything you want it to visible ⇒ put it in the body
- Head is your metadata ⇒ put everything that do not want to render

4. minimum requirement
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
  <head>
    <meta charset="utf-8">
    <title>title</title>
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">
    <script src="script.js"></script>
  </head>
  <body>
    <!-- page content -->
  </body>
</html>
